About Me

Hi, I'm Anna Smith, a certified birth and postpartum doula with over 7 years of experience. I believe every family deserves personalized, informed, and nurturing support. My approach is gentle, inclusive, and rooted in evidence-based practices. I support all families, birth choices, and backgrounds.

Services

  • Birth Doula Support: Continuous emotional & physical support during labor, comfort measures, birth plan preparation, and advocacy.
  • Postpartum Doula Support: Gentle in-home support for recovery, newborn care, feeding assistance, and light household help.
  • Prenatal Education: Private sessions on birth preparation, comfort techniques, and newborn care basics.
  • Virtual Support: Online consultations and guidance for families everywhere.
